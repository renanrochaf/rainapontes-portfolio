behind the lens

Raina Pontes, photographer, born in Fortaleza - Ceara is specialized in fashion, beauty and portraits. She works in the advertising and editorial area in partnership with large companies and agencies in Brazil.
At the age of eighteen she went to live in Paris, a trip that made her face different areas of photography, which inspired the creation of her first exhibition "Cinema das Almas", the result of this experience that was brought with her when she returned to Brazil

In her authorial work, Raina Pontes seeks to develop her personality through portraits, which are her great passion. With a particular aesthetic, the artist prints a more than personal signature on her work.
